Insight into how we make our products

We here at Eloheh take tremendous pride in ensuring that all drugs and treatment products are safe for use. We have a rigorous process that ensures the most effective and safe drugs for our customers.

Below is an outline of our process for making the safest and most effective drugs on the market.

  1. We start with Preclinical Testing in our laboratories to assess basic safety and biological activity
  2. We then move into Investigational New Drug (IND) Applications with regulatory authorities such as the FDA and global counterparts
  3. Once we get approval, Clinical development starts on the drug in 3 phases
    1. Phase I – testing the drug in small groups of healthy volunteers to evaluate safety, dosage, and pharmacokinetics
    2. Phase II – larger group testing to evaluate side effects and efficacy
    3. Phase III – even larger group, double-blind, randomized, placebo-controlled testing
  4. If the drug passes all 3 phases, we file a New Drug Application (NDA) with detailed trial results, manufacturing process, labeling, and prescribing info
  5. Once approved, we can market the drug to the public
  6. Even after market release, we continue ongoing monitoring and post-marketing surveillance to identify and address any new safety concerns
