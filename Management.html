Eloheh Leadership Team

Meet the visionary leaders driving healthcare innovation at Eloheh

Dr. Amara Johnson

Chief Operations Manager

Dr. Amara Johnson brings over 15 years of experience in healthcare operations and pharmaceutical development to Eloheh. As our Chief Operations Manager, she oversees the strategic implementation of our injectable and oral medication programs. Dr. Johnson holds an MBA in Healthcare Management from Northwestern University and a PharmD from Howard University. Her expertise in regulatory compliance and quality assurance has been instrumental in bringing our life-saving treatments from laboratory to market. She leads our cross-functional teams with a focus on patient-centered innovation and operational excellence.

Michael Chen

Director of Human Resources & Talent Development

Michael Chen serves as our Director of Human Resources, where he champions inclusive leadership and employee development across all Eloheh divisions. With a Master's degree in Organizational Psychology and 12 years of experience in healthcare HR, Michael specializes in building diverse, high-performing teams that drive our mission forward. He oversees talent acquisition, employee engagement, and leadership development programs for our growing workforce of over 500 healthcare professionals. His innovative approach to collaborative team building has resulted in industry-leading employee satisfaction scores and retention rates.

Dr. Ogechi Okonkwo

Global Strategy & Market Expansion Lead

Dr. Ogechi Okonkwo leads Eloheh's international expansion efforts as our Global Strategy & Market Expansion Lead. With a background in international business development and a PhD in Biomedical Engineering, Dr. Okonkwo has successfully launched our products in over 25 countries worldwide. He coordinates with regulatory bodies across different markets to ensure our innovative healthcare solutions meet global standards while maintaining our commitment to safety and efficacy. His strategic vision has positioned Eloheh as a trusted global partner in advancing healthcare accessibility and technology integration.
